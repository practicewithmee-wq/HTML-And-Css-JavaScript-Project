CAKE Recipe

The 7 Essential Ingredients

  • All-purpose flour (Maida): 1.5 cups
  • Granulated white sugar: 1 cup
  • Unsalted butter: ½ cup (softened to room temperature)
  • Eggs: 2 large (room temperature)
  • Milk: ½ cup (room temperature)
  • Vanilla extract: 2 teaspoons
  • Baking powder: 1.75 teaspoons
  • Salt: ¼ teaspoon

Rasgulla Recipe

The 4 Essential Ingredients

  • Sugar: 1.5 cups regular white sugar
  • Water: 4 cups water
  • Flavoring: 2-3 crushed green cardamoms or 1/4 teaspoon rose water (optional)
  • Ice Cubes: 10-15 cubes (for cooling the syrup and stopping the cooking process)

Dorayaki Recipe

The 9 Essential Ingredients

  • Eggs: 2-4 large eggs, which provide structure and fluffiness.
  • Sugar: Caster or granulated sugar, typically 1/4 to 1/2 cup depending on the batch size.
  • Honey: 1-2 tablespoons to give the pancakes their signature golden color and moist, spongy texture.
  • Mirin: 1 teaspoon (optional) for added depth of flavor and moisture.
  • Flour: 1 cup (or about 120-140g) of all-purpose flour or cake flour for a lighter crumb.
  • Leavening Agent: 1/2 to 1 teaspoon of baking powder or baking soda to make the pancakes rise.
  • Water: 1-4 tablespoons to adjust the batter to a pourable, silky consistency.
  • Neutral Oil: A tiny dash (about \(\frac{1}{2}\) tsp) for the batter, plus extra for lightly oiling the pan.
